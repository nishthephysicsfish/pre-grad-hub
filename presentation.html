Presentation Deliverance

Resources for Creating Award-Winning Presentations (Oral & Poster)

Delivering a standout presentation is a vital skill for college students—whether for class projects, research conferences, or poster sessions. This guide compiles top resources to help you design, prepare, and deliver presentations that captivate your audience and leave a lasting impression.

  • Purdue University – Showcase Guidelines and Resources for All Presenters
    Guidelines for oral and poster presentations, tips on recording, audio/video quality, and submission instructions.
    purdue.edu/conferences/events/ESLS/showcaseresources.php
  • Purdue Global Writing Center – Creating Effective PowerPoint® Presentations
    Step-by-step guide on structuring PowerPoint presentations, organizing content, and best practices for slides.
    purdueglobalwriting.center/creating-effective-powerpoint-presentations/
  • YouTube: Award-Winning Oral Presentation Example
    TEDx talks or university showcase winners for inspiration.
    Example: The 3 Magic Ingredients of Amazing Presentations – Phil Waknell
  • Reddit: r/PowerPoint and r/publicspeaking
    Community discussions, tips, and real-world advice from presenters and professionals.
    reddit.com/r/PowerPoint/
    reddit.com/r/publicspeaking/
  • Harvard Business Review – How to Give a Killer Presentation
    Techniques for engaging storytelling and memorable delivery.
    hbr.org/how-to-give-a-killer-presentation
  • Benjamin Ball Associates – 10 Top Presentation Tips
    Advice on storytelling, audience engagement, and interactive elements.
    benjaminball.com/blog/business-presentation-tips/
  • Crafting Award-Winning Presentations: Strategies from Champion Speakers
    Insights on storytelling, speechwriting, delivery, visuals, and technology.
    linkedin.com/pulse/crafting-award-winning-presentations-strategies-from-mba-gaicd-i7pqe
  • Montana State University – Engineering Communications Toolkit
    General guidelines for oral and poster presentations, tips for PowerPoint, and poster design.
    montana.edu/engcommtoolkit/presentation/
  • CEDAR Science – How to Give Award Winning Presentations (PDF)
    Tips on projection, articulation, visualizing your talk, and managing anxiety.
    cedarscience.org/L3.3_Lindsay_Goodwin.pdf
