Summer Research Portals

  • Pathways to Science
    A comprehensive directory of 600+ STEM summer research programs and scholarships for undergraduates, including NSF, NASA, NIH, and more.
    Visit Pathways to Science
  • NSF Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REU)
    Official National Science Foundation portal for REU Sites and Supplements across all research disciplines.
    Visit NSF REU
  • Council on Undergraduate Research (CUR) – Internship and Research Opportunities
    Searchable database of 600+ fully funded summer research programs for undergraduates in STEM fields.
    Visit CUR
  • REU Finder
    A specialized tool for locating NSF REU programs with application advice.
    Visit REU Finder
  • NIST Summer Undergraduate Research Fellowship (SURF)
    Competitive summer research program at the National Institute of Standards and Technology.
    Visit NIST SURF
  • NIH Summer Research Experience Program
    NIH-supported summer research opportunities for high school, undergraduate, and post-baccalaureate students in biomedical sciences.
    Visit NIH Summer Research
  • The Leadership Alliance Summer Research Early Identification Program (SR-EIP)
    National consortium offering summer research opportunities, especially for underrepresented students.
    Visit Leadership Alliance
  • Math REU Master List
    A comprehensive list of mathematics-focused REU programs for summer research.
    Visit Math REU Master List
  • Científico Latino REU Database
    Extensive, filterable database of 500+ summer research programs including REUs, SURPs, and SURFs.
    Visit Científico Latino REU Database
  • Amgen Scholars Program
    Prestigious summer research program in biomedical and life sciences at select US universities.
    Visit Amgen Scholars
